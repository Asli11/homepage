About me

I’m a web developer , specializing in frontend development and currently living in Germany.

My journey to web development started a couple years ago, when I actually got curious about game development. Since then I got to learn a lot of new technologies and design principles, which I really enjoyed. Still I am enjoying every new experience in development.

My main goal is to create products and experiences that feel good and make people happy. I’m a big fan of the open source community and like to share and participate with projects as much as I can. Currently I am working freelance and looking for new projects to built.
